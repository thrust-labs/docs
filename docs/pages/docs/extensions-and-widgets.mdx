# Enhancing Hubs with Extensions and Widgets

## Customization at Your Fingertips

Thrust's extension system introduces a world of possibilities for hubs, with widgets that provide access to custom information and functionalities.

### Versatile Widgets for Every Need

- **Diverse Use Cases**: Widgets support various use cases, both on-chain and off-chain, catering to the unique needs of your hub and its members.
- **Community-Created Content**: Anyone can create widgets, opening the door for community-driven innovations and personalized hub experiences.

Enhance your hub with the power and flexibility of Thrust's extensions and widgets.
