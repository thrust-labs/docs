# Revolutionizing Gaming with NFT Rentals

## Facilitating Lend and Rent Dynamics

Thrust introduces an NFT rental system, a game-changing feature for web3 gaming and guilds. This functionality allows users to lend and rent NFTs, offering new possibilities in gameplay and asset utilization.

### Empowering Players and Guilds

- **For Gamers**: Borrow NFTs for gaming, accessing assets that can enhance your gameplay experience.
- **For Guilds**: Lend out your NFT assets, enabling guild members to leverage them for their gaming needs.

This feature opens up a new realm of strategy and resource management, integral to the evolving landscape of web3 gaming.
