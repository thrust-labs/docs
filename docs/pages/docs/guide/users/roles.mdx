## Easy Addition and Role Assignment

- **Add New Users**: Quickly add new staff members to your backoffice with just a few clicks.
- **Manage Roles**: Assign and manage roles, tailoring permissions to suit different responsibilities within your team.

## Discord Integration

- **Discord Role Sync**: Leverage the bonus feature of pulling roles from Discord. Assign these roles to staff members within your hub, streamlining permissions and access based on your existing Discord setup.
